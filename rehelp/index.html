Who we are

Story about us

Video about us

ReHelp is a tokenized through ReHelp tokens real estate marketplace/labor fair. Essentially, the owners of real estate (residential or commercial) place adds for laborers needed, filling out a small questionnaire and describing in free form the desired result. The service providers (specialists in construction, repair, and maintenance) reply. The entire exchange is stored on a public blockchain, which ensures its transparency, reliability, and security. In order for our users to be able to optimize the financial side of their interaction, we propose to use (besides the usual pricing instruments - demand/supply, the market value of services, etc.) the auction model.

Why us

security

All the transactions are signified merely by a transaction record on the blockchain. By design, blockchains are inherently resistant to modification of the data.

Fair price

It has been proven mathematically that auctions are a socially acceptable and econ omnically effective pricing model, which can not be said about the classic market economy's principle of management by an "invisible hand".

Certification

Help team has set itself onto the task of creating a unified system of user certification of the quality services in real estate. The listing of certified service providers will also be stored in the ReHelp blockchain.

review system

feedback is recorded in the blockchain immediately after confirmation of the transaction for payment of services. This function is programmed with the implementation of a smart contract, which ensures that the author of the review has indeed been a client of the reviewed specialist.

Smart Contract

You no longer risk the down payment - a smart contract automatically charges you the amount prescribed in its terms, but the service provider will receive the funds only upon completion of work within the specified time, otherwise, you get your money back.

Auction system

The auction model allows a seller to receive, and a buyer to pay a purchase price that is as close to the true value of an object as possible in reference to both seller and buyer financial expectations.

The Vickrey auction (participants independently pr ce the lot but the winner is not the highest bidder but the second highest)

Auctioning of coupons (offers up for sale a certain amount of, or a particular kind of goods and services. This kind of a limited time offer will mostly benefit).

PRA, Price Review Auction (for the sale of the coupon for his services. If, however, the priority is making money, he can use the for offering up his services).

Penny auction (assumes zero initial value of the lot and a certain period of bidding. Each bet made me and that the lot price increases by some small amount ("penny"), and the auction period increases by a certain time interval ("time - plus"). The cost of the bid is much higher than the "penny" and often the seller's profit is determined by the amount earned from the bets made by the participants, while the final price of the lot is much lower than its market value. The winner is determined by the last bet made within a certain time interval)

Employing the auction method on our platform, the customer can thereby be sure that the system will automatically select only those who meet the criteria specified in the application (professional experience, certifications, reviews, rating) for participating in the auction. The service provider with the second least expensive price gets the contract.

Token Functionality

ReHelp tokens, like any other cryptocurrency, can be used as a means of payment on other platforms or as an investment. ReHelp tokens are created on the Ethereum platform and are based on the Ethereum protocol.


ReHelp tokens will be used across the ReHelp platform and will not be limited by the latform in the future. ReHelp coins will be used:

  • as a currency for ordering delivering the sales commission to the owners of the rights on digital property;
    for services rendered in ReHelp platform;
  • to promote your services on our platform;
  • to obtain a certificate of the quality;
  • to exchange your ReHelp tokens for another cryptocurrency or fiat currency via exchanges.

ReHelp users avoid from:

  • lirisk the down payment
  • bothering about the contractor's expenses
  • unscrupulous employers (thanks to escrow funds prescribed a smart contract)
  • drawing the lawyers (thanks to the smart contract software template)
